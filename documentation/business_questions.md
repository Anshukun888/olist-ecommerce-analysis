# Business Questions

This project is designed to answer the following business questions:

## Revenue & Sales
- What is the total revenue generated over time?
- How does monthly revenue trend change?

## Customer Analysis
- Who are the top customers by spending?
- How frequently do customers place orders?

## Product Analysis
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- What are the most frequently purchased products?

## Delivery Performance
- What is the average delivery time?
- How many orders are delivered late vs on time?

## Payment Analysis
- What are the most commonly used payment methods?
- Does payment type affect order value?

## Business Insights
- Which factors influence revenue growth?
- Where are the key improvement areas in logistics?
